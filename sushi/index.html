About Us

Work-study Program enables students of Bml Munjal University to do a paid work along with their studies. Students are provided with various job offers from different departments of this universities like IT department, accounts department, registrar office, marketing, library, registration team etc. Aim of this program is to nurture students of our university by enhancing advance and disseminate practically-oriented knowledge as by learning and working they will get experience which will help in future when they join any organization.
