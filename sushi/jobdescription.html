Mobile App Development Internship in Bangalore at Papertrell

Mobile App Development

Papertrell

Location(s): Bangalore

Start Date Duration Stipend Posted On Apply By
Immediately
3 Months 15000-20000 /Month 13 Dec'16 27 Dec'16
Part time
 
About Papertrell (http://www.papertrell.com):
Papertrell is an enterprise-grade interactive content platform that enables businesses to engage with their audience effectively. Leading publishers across the world use the Papertrell platform to go beyond simple digital replicas of print books to create truly interactive experiences that delight readers.
About the Internship:
The selected intern(s) will work on following during the internship:
1. Work with our app development team
2. Develop and deploy live modules
3. Android and iOS app development
# of Internships available: 2
Perks:
Certificate, Letter of recommendation, Pre placement offer (PPO), Flexible work hours, Informal dress code, 5 days a week, Free snacks & beverages.
Who can apply:
Only those candidates can apply who:
  1. are available for full time (in-office) internship.
  2. can start the internship between 13th Dec'16 and 12th Jan'17.
  3. are available for duration of 3 months (preferred, not mandatory).
  4. are pursuing any degree but have relevant skills and interest.
  5. are currently in any year of study or are recent graduates.
Other requirements:
  1. Skill(s) required: Java, HTML, JavaScript, C# and iOS and Android
